I am a roboticist working at Dexterity Inc. Before that, I spent 3 years as a Robotics Researcher working with Prof. Maxim Likhachev at the Search-based Planning Lab (SBPL) of Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. Prior to CMU, I was an undergaute student advised by Prof. Yingxiang Liu at the State Key Laboratory of Robotics and System, Harbin Institute of Technology.

Currently, I am dedicated to designing advanced motion planners for robot navigation and manipulation. Specifically, I am interested in designing algorithms which lie at the intersection between learning and planning.
