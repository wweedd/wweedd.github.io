About Me

I am a roboticist at Search-based Planning Lab, the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. I am advised by Prof. Maxim Likhachev.

My research interests lie at the intersection between motion planning and machine learning. Specifically, I am foucusing on the following:

  • Leveraging domain information through learning techniques to guide motion planners.
  • Designing robust planners by encoding world uncertainty into planning model via learning techniques.
