About Me

Research Interests:
My research interests lie at learning for motion planning. On the whole, I am concerned about the following problems: Firstly, classical motion planners, such as MHA*, BIT*, are not intelligent enough due to the limitations of heuristics that guide planners. Secondly, learned planners such as Motion Planning Networks does not generalize well. Therefore, they degrade to the low level embeded classical planners, if any, when applied to unknown scenarios. To this end, I am striving to bridge the gaps between experiences and new environments, thereby designing intelligent planners by:

  • Learning to encode uncertainties of environments.
  • Learning to estimate the interactions of the robot with collision objects or other agents.
  • Designing planners that incorporates aforementioned learned models.
